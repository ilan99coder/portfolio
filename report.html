My Experience

Creating my first website portfolio was an exciting experience. At first, I thought it would be a boring task, but it turned out to be a lot more fun, especially when I got to choose my own aesthetic palette. Of course, there were moments when small errors popped up, and it felt like everything was falling apart. Trying to figure out where it went wrong was draining at times, but thanks to Chris, my tutor, everything became so much easier. His guidance really helped me get through those tough moments and made the whole process enjoyable.
